About Our University

Founded in 1869 in Bucharest, the “Carol Davila” University of Medicine and Pharmacy is one of the oldest educational institutions in Romania. Historically, it is related to numerous enlightened scientists such as Victor Babeș, Francisc Rainer and Thoma Ionescu, who chose to share their amazing knowledge to the future medical practitioners.

About Our University

Nowadays, it is recognised to be one of the best medical universities in Europe, not only for its students' impressive results, but also for the constant involvement in national and international medical projects. Due to the collaboration with the SSMB and RSSS, the university created new educational opportunities and linked Romania to the international medical world.

About Our University

The "Carol Davila" University of Medicine and Pharmacy hosts the headquarters of the SSMB, supporting many of our projects. The Faculty of Medicine is characterised by equity, high educational standards and multiculturalism, allowing the society to get involved in the process of improving students' conditions. The University is part of the International Medical Students' Congress of Bucharest, supporting the event by all means.
