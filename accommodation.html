Accommodation

Hello Hotels is classified by European standards as a 2 star hotel, having a modern and practical design. The hotel welcomes its guests in modern, 15 square meters rooms equipped with double beds, LCD television sets, free Wi-Fi internet access, air conditioning and close-circuit telephone. The working desks, together with the fresh air and large windows make the rooms ideal for students. Other great features of Hello Hotels are the restaurant, bar and coffee shop which all guests have access to.

One of its greatest advantages is the excellent location, being situated 1 minute away from the closest subway station and 3 minutes walking distance from Bucharest’s main train station (Gara de Nord). The hotel is located approximately 20 km from the “Henri Coandă” International Airport, the two being connected by the Express 780 Bus.

Hello Hotels is also close to the participants’ main areas of interest – the “Carol Davila” University of Medicine and Pharmacy, the Old Center, the Victory square. At the same time, being located in one of the most central areas, guests can easily find their way around by any means of transportation.

The Accommodation Package (75€) includes 4 nights of accommodation in a double room, with a roommate of your choice, as well as breakfast. Make sure you check “Accommodation” in your account, select your “Accommodation Buddy” and, after depositing the fee, upload the proof of payment in the corresponding section of your account.
