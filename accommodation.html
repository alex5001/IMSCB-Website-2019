Accommodation

As students coming to a foreign country, or even from a different city of Romania, we know that it may be hard to find a good accomodation regarding comfort, location and fees as well. But, the International Medical Students’ Congress of Bucharest’s organising committee wishes that all of you will have a pleasant and memorable experience here in Bucharest. With this aim we will look up a few hotels in our area and we will come back with a presentation of the hotel we find appropiate, as soon as we can!

One of its greatest advantages is the excellent location, being situated 1 minute away from the closest subway station and 3 minutes walking distance from Bucharest’s main train station (Gara de Nord). The hotel is located approximately 20 km from the “Henri Coandă” International Airport, the two being connected by the Express 780 Bus.

Hello Hotels is also close to the participants’ main areas of interest – the “Carol Davila” University of Medicine and Pharmacy, the Old Center, the Victory square. At the same time, being located in one of the most central areas, guests can easily find their way around by any means of transportation.

The Accommodation Package (70€) includes 4 nights of accommodation in a double room, with a roommate of your choice, as well as breakfast. Make sure you check “Accommodation” in your account, select your “Accommodation Buddy” and, after depositing the fee, upload the proof of payment in the corresponding section of your account.
