Accommodation

As students coming to a foreign country, or even from a different city of Romania, we know that it may be hard to find a good accomodation regarding comfort, location and fees as well. But, the International Medical Students’ Congress of Bucharest’s organising committee wishes that all of you will have a pleasant and memorable experience here in Bucharest. With this aim we will look up a few hotels in our area and we will come back with a presentation of the hotel we find appropriate, as soon as we can!
