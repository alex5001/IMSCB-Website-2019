Opening Ceremony

The 3rd edition of the IMSCB will welcome all the curious ones that have accepted the challenge of science and knowledge in an exquisite enviroment combining a sparkle of elegance with a pinch of style.

Location

Dedicated to one of the best-known medical figures in history, Ana Aslan, the Opening Ceremony will be held in a sanctuary of culture and innovation: The National Institute of Geriatrics and Gerontology “Ana Aslan”. Come to meet the team, listen to different inspiring stories collected from all over the world and enjoy an unforgettable evening in a magic scenery. The whole experience will be completed by a charming cocktail-party that will surely leave a long-lasting stamp on your memory.

Founded in 1952 the Institute of Gerontology and Geriatrics was the first of its kind in the whole world. A couple of years after, in 1992 it was named after the amazing professor Ana Aslan. From the first days of existence the main purpose of the institute was to provide medical assistance but also to discover new anti-aging techniques to promote the results worldwide.

In 1964 the president of the World Health Organization promoted the institute as a model for every country, being the only one that was so advanced in this area. Many research and clinical studies were made throughout the years in the "Ana Aslan" Institute of Gerontology and Geriatrics and this is why nowadays it is still a model in this field.
