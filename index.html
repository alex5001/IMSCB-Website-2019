The International Medical Students' Congress of Bucharest

Welcome to the third edition of the International Medical Students' Congress of Bucharest, the annual Congress of the Medical Students' Society of Bucharest.

Join us, from the 4th to the 8th of December 2019, in Bucharest, Romania, for one of the greatest medical students’ congresses in Europe.

Don't miss the opportunity to attend inspiring conferences, train your medical skills during our workshops and meet medical students from all around the world!

Who supports the IMSCB?

Created over 20 years ago, from the pure desire of the romanian medical students to share their knowledge and skills all over the world, the International Medical Students’ Congress of Bucharest is extending its borders globally for the 3rd time, between the 4th and 8th of December 2019.

Dedicated in honour of the empowering female medical pioneer Ana Aslan, the event is built on a strong belief in multiculturality and multidisciplinarity. Consequently, we have prepared numerous conferences and keynotes, held by both national and international speakers, on a large variety of medical fields that can suit your interest! We promise you a wide range of unique and professional hands-on workshops, held in a friendly enviroment.

Don’t miss the amazing opportunity to discover the universe of medicine starting from neurology, cardiology, biomedicine, genetics to forensic medicine. We have a place for each and every one of you!

IMSCB values students’ and young doctors’ involvement in the medical process and as a result, we encourage you to take a step into the full experience provided by our congress, by registering as an active participant with your own abstract. Your hard work will be rewarded accordingly!

Our efforts have been cosntantly supported by the amazing parteners: Carpatica, Domeniile Sâmburești, Alphasigma and many more that decided to stand next to us in order to provide you the best experience. Intertwining the passion of medicine with the will to offer students’ the opportunity to broaden their knowledge and train their medical skills, the IMSCB is the result of a long working proces.

Last but not least, one of our goals is to connect medical students all over the world, offering them the opportunity not only to expand their knowledge and practical skills, but also to discover new cultures. Therefore, we have prepared for you a magnificent social programme, including a fine selection of the most authentic locations that will reveal you the wonders of Romania.
