About SSMB

Founded on the 13th of January 1875, the Medical Student's Society of Bucharest (SSMB) is nowadays one of the largest NGOs, representing more than 8000 individuals.

For decades, the original aim of the organisation has been carefully preserved with numerous new objectives being added. The main purposes of SSMB are to create valuable and equitable opportunities for students, to facilitate both the process of teaching and learning and to promote multiculturalism and social involvement.

About SSMB

Nowadays, the organisation is anchored both nationally and internationally, being included in 2 national federations, as well as the IFMSA (the International Federation of Medical Students’ Associations). Taking these big steps further from the initial remote group, the organisation is linked today to a great network, creating successful events dedicated to the community.

The organisation also ensures that students' rights and responsibilities are equally distributed and respected, being involved in the decisional process of the “Carol Davila” University of Medicine and Pharmacy.

About SSMB

Apart from being a voice of the young medical generation, the SSMB is responsible of over 50 local and national projects each year, all of which handle various issues relevant to the 21 st century. The Association organises biannual blood donation campaigns, free Basic Life Support workshops, scientific events dedicated to medical students, school presentations on public and reproductive health issues, fundraisers for children’s hospitals and many other activities, all with the help of its one thousand volunteers.
