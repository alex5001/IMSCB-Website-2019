About IMSCB

The International Medical Students' Congress of Bucharest is annually organised by the Medical Student’s Society of Bucharest in December, keeping its tradition. The event dates back to 1997, when it was implemented as a national congress known as "The National Congress of Students and Young Physicians". Due to the visible interest shown by not only medical students, but also young doctors, after 20 successful editions we decided to take a step further, abroad.

About IMSCB

After 2 decades of updating and reinventing itself, the Congress dissolved the borders and created a long-lasting network over the whole world. Therefore, we are glad to receive registrations from each and every continent, bringing the medical community closer. At the same time, we feel thankful for the opportunity to put Romania on the global medical map, combining in our vast scientific programme excellent Romanian Professors and renowned international speakers.

About IMSCB

We are proud to say that the International Medical Students’ Congress of Bucharest offers not only the greatest number of scientific events of all European congresses, but also on the most diverse subjects, including conferences and workshops on most of the medical specialties. Besides the chance to take part in high-quality scientific events, we encourage you to take advantage of the opportunity to present your research in front of experienced doctors.

About IMSCB

Created with passion and care, the IMSCB is a yearly occasion not only to develop your medical knowledge, but also to achieve and practice your skills in any specialty. The congress offers the amazing chance to discover yourself, fructify your passion and learn to love the art of medicine.
