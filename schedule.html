The 3rd edition of the IMSCB brings you 2 different categories of hands-on workshops. Regardless of your choice, if you are a PASSIVE Participant to be eligible to get a diploma you need to attend 1 Workshop from each category, 1 Conference and 1 Keynote. On the other hand, ACTIVE Participants must attend only 1 Workshop from each category and 1 Keynote. Because we would like to give all of you the opportunity to broaden your knowledge, each and ever one of you who is interested in a Keynote will have free access, without the need of completing any online form in advance.
