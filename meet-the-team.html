Meet the team

We know that every great thing in life is made with hard work and devotion and those two are the qualities that gathered this great individuals under one word: TEAM. Our goal is to offer you the best experience at The International Medical Students’ Congress of Bucharest 3rd edition.

Passion, creativity and numerous hours of work are put into every step of the process by our amazing Organising Committee, making sure that this Congress will be a medical experience worthy for your future self.
