The Annual International Medical Students Meeting

The Annual International Medical Students Meeting is an international medical congress hosted in Lisbon, entirely held by students every year in March. With renowned speakers and a huge range of practical workshops, this is one of the best in Europe. Each day is based on a subject module and all the scientific lectures related to it. Besides these, there are also keynote lectures, clinical scientific and research competitions and a science speed dating event. Join us in Lisbon from the 26th to the 29th of March 2020 for our 11th edition!

Airlangga Medical Scientific Week (AMSW)

Airlangga Medical Scientific Week (AMSW) is an annual scientific event which opens opportunities for medical students in Indonesia and around the world to submit their works in the form of research papers, literature reviews, scientific essays, public posters and education videos. They also organise non-competition programs like International Symposium (open to public), Gala dinner, and City tour. This year’s theme is “Emergency Medicine: a Comprehensive Approach to Build Safe Community”.

ZIMS

ZIMS is a medical congress that brings together medical students and young doctors worldwide. It is one of the few conferences where young students and not yet affirmed scientist have access to publishing their work, thus becoming the only conference in Europe that publishes the abstracts book as a supplement to a prestigious medical journal, ‘Liječnički vjesnik’, which is indexed in EMBASE/Index Medicus. Moreover, the best works will be available for the readers as full texts.
